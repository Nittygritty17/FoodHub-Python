Question 14: The company charges the restaurant 25% on the orders having cost greater than 20 dollars and 15% on the orders having cost greater than 5 dollars. Find the net revenue generated by the company across all orders. [3 marks]¶

# Write the code here
#Apply commission rates earned by the company
def commission_rate(cost):
  if cost > 20:
    return cost * 0.25
  elif cost > 5:
    return cost * 0.15
  else:
    return 0.0

#Apply function to each order
df['commission'] = df['cost_of_the_order'].apply(commission_rate)

#Sum the total revenue
total_revenue = df['commission'].sum()

#Result
print(f"Total net revenue generated by the company: ${total_revenue:.2f}")

Conclusion and Recommendations¶

Question 17: What are your conclusions from the analysis? What recommendations would you like to share to help improve the business? (You can use cuisine type and feedback ratings to drive your business recommendations.) [6 marks]¶

Conclusions:¶

  • As per the results retrieved from the given data we can conclude that -
  • By Cuisine type -

  1. As per the univariate analysis, American cuisine is the most popular cuisine, especially on weekends, followed by Japanese and Mexican. Hence, these cuisines contribute significantly in the company's revenue as the order count is good and hence, good commission earned from the restaurants.

  2. As per the multivariate analysis, Cuisines like Japanese and Mexican have higher average order costs, which in return give more commission to the company.

  • By Weekdays and Weekends - More orders are placed on weekends as compared to weekdays.
  • By Customer Rating

  1. Out of total 1898 orders placed, there are no ratings given by the customers for 736 orders. Due to which we cannot guage the experience of the cutomers for the given orders.

  2. Out of the total ratings given, ratings 5 and 4 are in majority which further signifies customer satisfaction.

  3. Lower ratings are given for orders that have longer delivery time which signifies that customer experience gets impacted due to delivery time.

  4. As per analysis of restaurants by rating count and rating, there are 4 reliable restaurants with rating count above 50 and rating greater than 4. These are eligble ones for promotional offers.

Recommendations:¶

As per the conclusions from the given dataset, I would recommend that -¶

Highlight popular cuisines

  1. We can offer deals and discounts on popular cuisines like American and Japanese to increase order count and value especially on weekends as more orders are placed on weekends.
  2. Market cuisine themed promotions on weekends.
  3. Offer discounts and deals as per the festivities of a cuisnine by country holidays to increase emotional value of good chunk of customers to enhance long term relationship with the customers.
  4. Promote premium add-ons on for popular cuisines to push orders above $20 bracket and increase commission.

Promote Restaurants with good ratings and order count

  1. Market restaurants who offer good services (Average rating above 4 and orders above 50)to the customers. This will encourage and build good repo with valuable vendors and enhance customer trust and satisfaction.

Encourage customers to give ratings

  1. Introduce reward points or coupons for feedback given for orders and delivery experience so that its easy to differ the food quality experience and delivery experience separately. This will help to analyse and improve the relevant service improvement in depth.

Delivery Time Experience

  1. Focus is required on reducing delivery time as most orders are rated between 1-3 due to longer delivery timings.

Premium membership Services
